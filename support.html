Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How do I reset my password?

A: You can reset your password by visiting the login page and clicking on the "Forgot Password" link. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email address.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Please refer to our billing section for a complete list.

Q: How can I upgrade my hosting plan?

A: To upgrade your hosting plan, log in to your client area and navigate to the "Services" section. Select the plan you wish to upgrade and follow the upgrade prompts.

Q: My website is down, what should I do?

A: First, check your internet connection. If that's not the issue, please contact our support team immediately via any of the methods above, providing as much detail as possible (e.g., error messages, recent changes).

If you can't find the answer to your question here, please don't hesitate to contact us directly.
